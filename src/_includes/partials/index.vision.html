Celeste’s Vision for Utica

Since being sworn into office on January 1, 2020, I have worked hard to bring transparent, accessible, and responsive representation to the people of Utica’s Third Ward.

My commitment to the goals of robust democracy have brought me into regular contact with my Constituents. I have resolved dozens upon dozens of Constituents’ issues, from the need for new stop signs to help get problem trees cut down. I have consistently brought to the Council’s attention the need to be accountable and transparent to the voters of Utica.

I am excited to be running for reelection in 2021. I need your support in order to continue fighting to make Utica a place where everyone can thrive and be heard and know that they are represented in our democracy. Please join me!
