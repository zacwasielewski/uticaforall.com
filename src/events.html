Candidate Meet-and-Greet

Thursday, August 12th, 5–7pm

On Thursday, August 12th, The Utica Third Ward Democratic Committee will be sponsoring a meet and greet gathering here at 37 Emerson Ave. You will be able to meet and get to know two judicial candidates who will be on the ballot this November: Karen Stanislaus who is running for Oneida County Family Court Judge and our favorite former Congressman, Anthony Brindisi, who is running for NYS Supreme Court Justice for the Fifth Judicial District.
